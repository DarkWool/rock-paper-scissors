In rock-paper-scissors, you have to randomly choose one of the three images above that represents: rock, paper and scissors respectively, you are going to play against the machine.

Here are the rules that determine which sign beats another:

  1. Rock wins over scissors.
  2. Paper wins over rock.
  3. Scissors wins over paper.
